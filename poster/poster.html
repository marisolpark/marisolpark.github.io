SPIRIT FINGERS

This Webtoon series is a coming of age story centered around the character Amy Jang. Amy lives as the outcast of her strict family and is bullied by some of her classmates in school, thus has no confidence in herself and lives a life that she does not enjoy. The story follows the journey Amy's experinces when joining a drawing club called "Spiritt Fingers" where she meets and interacts with multiple people with different personalities. Through this journey Amy is able to grow as a person and discovers how to love herself, her passions, and her goals in life.
